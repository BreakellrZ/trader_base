F.A.Q

Firstly TraderBase is free. When you sign up to TraderBase you unlock all the websites features. These features include : My Personal Trading Journal - my journal consists of Daily Analysis, Weekly Analysis, Pyschology, models & more. Within each Journal post you can comment on each post and ask any questions you may have. Alongside my trading Journal, you will unlock the ability to view the checklist feature.

The checklist feature is to be used for trades. In the checklist feature you can add anything you like to your own personal trade checklist. All you have to do is type into the input bar and click submit. Your checklist will then be updated. You can always update each input you put in, and you can also delte each input. When you are going through your checklist, if you click 'update' you can update your input, here you can also click the 'complete' button. Once you do this the input will be crossed out so it tells you that you have checked of that input for your trade checklist.
